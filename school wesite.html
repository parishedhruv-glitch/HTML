St Francis, Melton West

Why is it named st francis

The school is named st francis because there was a st named francis. He was a wealthy italian teen who abandoned a life of luxury to embrace poverty, service, and a deep love to nature and animals.

what are the key values our school represents

Empathy
Empathy is when you understand how someone else feels. For example "putting your self in someone elses shoes" so you can see things from their perspective.

Endeavour
Endeavour means to try very hard or make a serious effort to achieve a goal, especially when the task is difficult or important.

Community
Community is when a group of students and teachers whos responsibility is to create a safe, supportive, inclusive and encouraging learning space to learn

Subjects

In St francis we have the following subjects. English, Math, Religon, Physical Education, Health, Science, Art, Music, Drama, Woodwork, Italian, Auslan, Food Tech, and we have Technology.

Periods

School starts at 8:40am and ends at 3pm. Every student must go to their designated homeroom so their teachers can mark their attendence. After homeroom finishes you will go to your 1st period. Each period goes for an 1hr and 10 minutes. after 1st period is done we will go to our 2nd, then after that finishes all students have lunch break which goes for 50 minutes. After lunch break we have 3rd period, then after it finishes we have a snack break which lasts for 25 minutes. After scnack break we have our last period, then our school day ends.
